Uzbek cuisine is known for dishes like plov, lagman, shashlik, samsa, and fresh bread called non. Meals often include lamb, rice, vegetables, and rich spices.
Uzbekistan has a population of over 37 million people, making it one of the most populated countries in Central Asia.
The majority religion in Uzbekistan is Islam, mainly Sunni Muslim. Smaller communities of Orthodox Christians, Jews, and other religions also live there.
Uzbek is the official language. Russian is also widely spoken, especially in cities, and some people speak Tajik and other regional languages.
